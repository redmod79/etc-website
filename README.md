# The Final Fate of the Wicked: What Does the Bible Teach?

A comprehensive 18-study biblical investigation examining every major text, word, and argument bearing on the final fate of the wicked.

**View the site: https://redmod79.github.io/etc-website/**

## Studies

| # | Study | Question |
|---|-------|----------|
| 01 | What Is a Human Being? | What does Genesis 2:7 describe? What are nephesh, ruach, neshamah? |
| 02 | Who Has Immortality? | Does every human inherently possess immortality, or must it be received? |
| 03 | How Does the Bible Define Death? | Is death the separation of an immortal soul from the body, or something else? |
| 04 | What Is the Condition of the Dead? | Are the dead conscious or unconscious? |
| 05 | The Four "Hell" Words | What do Sheol, Hades, Gehenna, and Tartarus mean? |
| 06 | Destruction Vocabulary | What do "perish," "destroy," "consume," and "cut off" mean? |
| 07 | Olam: "Forever" in the OT | Does "forever" always mean infinite, endless duration? |
| 08 | Aionios: "Eternal" in the NT | What do aion and aionios mean? |
| 09 | The Rich Man and Lazarus | What is Luke 16:19-31 teaching? |
| 10 | Souls Under the Altar | What are the "souls under the altar" in Revelation 6:9-11? |
| 11 | Smoke Ascending Forever | What does Revelation 14:9-11 describe? |
| 12 | Tormented Day and Night Forever | What does Revelation 20:10 mean? |
| 13 | The Lake of Fire and Second Death | What is the "lake of fire"? What is the "second death"? |
| 14 | The Five Judgment Passages | Matt 25:46, Mark 9:43-48, Matt 10:28, 2 Thess 1:9, Dan 12:2 |
| 15 | The Strongest Case for ECT | What is the strongest biblical case for eternal conscious torment? |
| 16 | Historical Origins of ECT | Where did the concept of an inherently immortal soul come from? |
| 17 | God's Character and Justice | Does the Bible describe God's justice as proportional or unlimited? |
| 18 | Comprehensive Synthesis | Taking ALL the evidence together, where does the weight fall? |
