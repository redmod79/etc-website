### Key OT parallel: Leviticus 17:11 — "the life of the flesh is in the blood; and I have given it to you upon the altar" — the life/soul (nephesh) is in the blood, and the blood is poured out at the base of the altar. ### This OT background (Lev 17:11; Gen 4:10 — Abel's blood crying from the ground) significantly affects how to read Rev 6:9. The "souls" crying under the altar may be a figurative representation of the martyrs' blood crying for vindication (a well-established OT metaphor), not necessarily literal conscious disembodied entities.
