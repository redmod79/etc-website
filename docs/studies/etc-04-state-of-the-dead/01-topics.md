### Research Notes

**Key Nave's observations for this study:**

1. DEATH is listed as "Called SLEEP" with a broad list: DEU 31:16; JOB 7:21; 14:12; JER 51:39; DAN 12:2; JHN 11:11; ACT 7:60; 13:36; 1CO 15:6,18,51; 1TH 4:14,15 — a systematic NT use of sleep-language for death.

2. DEAD (PEOPLE) UNCLASSIFIED lists include JOB 3:13-19; PSA 88:10-12; 115:17; ECC 9:5,6 — the consciousness-at-death question texts.

3. DEATH OF THE RIGHTEOUS includes both LUK 23:43 (paradise) and 1TH 4:13,14 (sleep), and 2CO 5:1,4,8 (absent from body) — these are the passages on both sides of IB001.

4. IMMORTALITY references ECC 3:21 and 12:7 alongside resurrection texts, but the references span both soul-sleep and conscious-state traditions without resolution.

5. HADES entry #2 (the realm of the dead) groups together Ps 115:17, Ecc 9:4-6, Ps 88:10-12 (unconsciousness texts) with Luke 23:43 and 2 Cor 12:4 (paradise texts) under the same heading — Nave himself groups them as all relating to "the realm of the dead."
