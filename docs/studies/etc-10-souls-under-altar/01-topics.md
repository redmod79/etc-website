## Topics

### ALTAR
- Built by Noah (Gen 8:20), Abraham (Gen 12:7,8; 13:18; 22:9), Isaac (Gen 26:25), Jacob (Gen 33:20; 35:1-7), Moses (Exo 17:15; 24:4), Balaam (Num 23:1,14,29), Joshua (Deu 27:4-7; Jos 8:30-32), the Reubenites and Gadites (Jos 22:10,34), Gideon (Jdg 6:26,27), Samuel (1Sa 7:17), Saul (1Sa 14:35), David (2Sa 24:18,19), Elijah (1Ki 18:31,32)
- Mosaic commandments prescribing the construction of: Exo 20:24-26; Deu 27:5-7; Jos 8:30,31; Eze 43:13
- Used in idolatrous worship: Jdg 6:25; 1Ki 12:32; 16:32; 18:26; 2Ki 16:10; 23:12,15; Isa 27:9; 65:3; Hos 8:11; Act 17:23
- **OF BURNT OFFERINGS**
  - Called BRAZEN ALTAR (Exo 39:39; 1Ki 8:64), ALTAR OF GOD (Psa 43:4), ALTAR OF THE LORD (Mal 2:13)
  - **IN THE TABERNACLE:** Pattern (Exo 27:1-8), Constructed by Bezaleel (Exo 38:1-7), Location (Exo 40:6,29; Eze 8:16; Mat 23:35), Furniture (Exo 27:3-7; 38:3-7; 1Sa 2:13,14), Horns (Exo 27:2), Uses of the horns (Psa 118:27)
  - How sanctified: Exo 29:36,37,44; 30:26-28; 40:10; Lev 8:10,11; Num 7
  - Sanctified everything that touched it: Exo 29:37; 30:29; Mat 23:18,19
  - A place of refuge: Exo 21:14; 1Ki 1:50; 2:28
  - **IN SOLOMON'S TEMPLE:** Description (2Ch 4:1), Renewed by Asa (2Ch 15:8), Removed by Ahaz (2Ki 16:14-17), Cleansed by Hezekiah (2Ch 29:18-24), Repaired by Manasseh (2Ch 33:16), Furniture taken to Babylon (2Ki 25:14), In second temple (Ezr 3:1-6), Ezekiel's vision (Eze 43:13-27)
- **OF INCENSE**
  - Called the GOLDEN ALTAR (Exo 39:38; Num 4:11), ALTAR OF SWEET INCENSE (Lev 4:7), ALTAR BEFORE THE LORD (Lev 16:18)
  - Pattern: Exo 30:1-5; Constructed: Exo 37:25-28; Location: Exo 30:6; 40:5,26
  - Uses: Exo 30:7-10,26,27; 40:27; Lev 4:7,18; 8:15; 9:9; 16:12,18
  - In Solomon's temple: 1Ki 6:19,20; 7:48; 1Ch 28:18
  - **Seen in John's vision: Rev 8:3; 9:13**

### BLOOD
