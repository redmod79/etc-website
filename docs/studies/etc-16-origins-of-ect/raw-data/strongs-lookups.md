# Raw Strong's Lookups

## G110 - athanasia
Transliteration: athanasia
POS: feminine noun
Definition: from a-thanatos; deathlessness, immortality
Occurrences: 3x (1 Cor 15:53, 54; 1 Tim 6:16)

## G861 - aphtharsia
Transliteration: aphtharsia
POS: feminine noun
Definition: incorruptibility; genitive, unending existence
Occurrences: 8x (Rom 2:7; 1 Cor 15:42; Eph 6:24; 2 Tim 1:10; Tit 2:7)

## G862 - aphthartos
Transliteration: aphthartos
POS: adjective
Definition: undecaying, incorruptible
Translations: "incorruptible" (2x), "uncorruptible" (1x), "immortal" (1x)

## G5590 - psyche
Transliteration: psyche
POS: feminine noun
Definition: from psycho (to breathe); breath, spirit, soul
Occurrences: 105x
Major translations: "soul," "life," "mind," "heart"

## G2349 - thnetos
Transliteration: thnetos
POS: adjective
Definition: from thnesko; liable to die, mortal
Occurrences: 6x
Translations: "mortal" (5x), "mortality" (1x)

## G2288 - thanatos
Transliteration: thanatos
POS: masculine noun
Definition: death (literally or figuratively)
Occurrences: 89x
Translations: "death" (55x), "of death" (26x), etc.

## G2222 - zoe
Transliteration: zoe
POS: feminine noun
Definition: from zao; life
Occurrences: 80x
Translations: "life" (54x), "of life" (24x), etc.
