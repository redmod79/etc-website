# Topics Research

## Question

What does "death" mean in the Bible? Is death cessation of existence, separation of soul from body, or separation from God? What Hebrew and Greek words are translated "death"? What is the origin of death? Is death reversible? What is the "second death"?

### HELL (Nave's Entry — relevant portion)

**Nave's Topical Bible — HELL**

(In the A.V. this word occurs in O.T. Scriptures and is the translation of the Hebrew word "sheol," which signifies the unseen state)

In the R.V. of O.T. it appears only in: Isa 5:14; 14:9, 15; 28:15, 18; 57:9; Ezk 31:16, 17; 32:21, 27; Amos 9:2; Jon 2:2; Hab 2:5

In the R.V., "sheol" is translated "lowest pit" (Deut 32:22; Psalm 86:13) and "pit" (Psalm 55:15)

Sheol is translated "grave" in A.V. in: Gen 37:35; 42:38; 44:29, 31; 1 Sam 2:6; 1 Kings 2:6, 9; Job 7:9; 14:13; 17:13; 21:13; 24:19; Psalm 6:5; 30:3; 31:17; 49:14, 15; 88:3; 89:48; 141:7; Prov 1:12; 30:16; Ecc 9:10; Song 8:6; Hos 13:14

The Greek word "gehenna" is translated "hell" in: Matt 5:22, 29, 30; 10:28; 18:9; 23:15, 33; Mark 9:43, 45, 47; Luke 12:5; Jas 3:6

The R.V. has introduced "Hades," the word found in the Greek text, which signifies the unseen world, in: Matt 11:23; 16:18; Luke 10:15; 16:23; Acts 2:27, 31; Rev 1:18; 6:8; 20:13, 14

**The Future Home of the Wicked:**
Psalm 9:17; Prov 5:5; 9:13, 15-18; 15:24; 23:13, 14; Isa 30:33; 33:14; Matt 3:12; 5:29, 30; 7:13, 14; 8:11, 12; 10:28; 13:30, 38-42, 49, 50; 16:18; 18:8, 9, 34, 35; 22:13; 25:28-30, 41, 46; Mark 9:43-48; Luke 3:17; 16:23-26, 28; Acts 1:25; 2 Thess 1:9; 2 Pet 2:4; Jude 1:6-23; Rev 2:11; 9:1, 2; 11:7; 14:10, 11; 19:20; 20:10, 15; 21:8 — See WICKED, PUNISHMENT OF
