## Revelation 4:8

**Greek Text:** καὶ τὰ τέσσερα ζῷα, ἓν καθ' ἓν αὐτῶν ἔχων ἀνὰ πτέρυγας ἕξ, κυκλόθεν καὶ ἔσωθεν γέμουσιν ὀφθαλμῶν· καὶ ἀνάπαυσιν οὐκ ἔχουσιν ἡμέρας καὶ νυκτὸς λέγοντες Ἅγιος ἅγιος ἅγιος Κύριος ὁ Θεός ὁ Παντοκράτωρ, ὁ ἦν καὶ ὁ ὢν καὶ ὁ ἐρχόμενος.

**KEY NOTES:**
- ἀνάπαυσιν οὐκ ἔχουσιν ἡμέρας καὶ νυκτὸς = "they have no rest (anapausin ouk ekhousin) day and night"
- This is **grammatically identical** to Rev 14:11's "they have no rest (anapausin ouk ekhousin) day nor night"
  - Same noun G372 (ἀνάπαυσιν), same negative (οὐκ), same verb G2192 (ἔχουσιν), same genitive time expression
- λέγοντες = Present Active Participle — their "not resting" is expressed AS continuous activity: "saying, Holy, holy, holy"
- The "no rest" of Rev 4:8 = unceasing positive worship activity

**KEY NOTES for Jude 1:7:**
- πρόκεινται = "they lie before/are set forth as" — Present M/P Indicative, 3rd plural — ongoing exemplary status
- δεῖγμα (G1164) = "example, specimen" — Sodom/Gomorrah ARE (present tense) a specimen
- πυρὸς αἰωνίου = **genitive singular of G4442 + G166** — "of eternal fire" (genitive phrase qualifying δίκην)
- δίκην ὑπέχουσαι = "undergoing punishment" — Present Active Participle — Sodom/Gomorrah ARE (still) undergoing eternal fire
- G166 (aiōnios) modifies pyr (fire) — the fire itself is called "eternal/age-lasting"
- CRITICAL: The Sodom cities were turned to ashes (2 Pet 2:6). The "eternal fire" that destroyed them is described by Jude as their ongoing punishment IN THE SENSE that they remain destroyed — their destruction is permanent and exemplary. The fire is not still physically burning.
