## 1 Samuel 1:22 — Samuel to Abide Le-olam (Ad-olam variant)

```
======================================================================
  1 Samuel 1:22
======================================================================

  [Key phrase:]
  וְ יָ֥שַׁב שָׁ֖ם עַד־עֹולָֽם
  = "and he shall dwell there ad-olam"
  = unto-eternity

  Hebrew terms:
  עַד (ad) = prep "unto/until"
  עֹולָֽם (olam) = eternity

  Verb: יָ֥שַׁב (yashav) = sit/dwell
```

**Analysis:**
- עַד עֹולָם (ad-olam) = "unto eternity" — a prepositional phrase using H5703 (ad) as preposition + H5769 (olam) as noun
- Applied to Samuel's lifelong service at the tabernacle
- Samuel died (1 Sam 25:1), confirming olam is bounded by the subject's mortal existence
